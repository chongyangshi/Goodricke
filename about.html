About


GNU/Goodricke was built by chongyangshi, in order to resolve the issue of him constantly forgetting to prepare for kitchen cleaning days.

It is named after Goodricke College's central nucleus, with a building code of "GNU".

You can view the source code here, GPLv2 licensed.







Frequently Asked Questions


Who provides this service?

I, chongyangshi, a Computer Science student, made this service as an interest project to remind myself of public kitchen cleaning days, when I was living in a hall of residence on campus. This has now been developed into a public platform that anyone can use to set their own email reminders for cleaning times. This service is hosted with my own resources in the public Internet.


Do you offer any guarantees on reminders?

Unfortunately, no. Although I try my best to maintain this service available at all times, unforeseen consequences could, in theory, happen to web servers or to the university's email system. Delivery of email reminders are also subject to university email system's approval.


I have set up a wrong subscription! What do I do now?

When you receive a reminder, you can remove your email from the system by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in the notification email. You will then be able to set up a new one.


What if someone else set up a subscription with my email address?

As long as you do not click on the approval link in your confirmation email, you will not receive any email reminders, and your email address cannot be used to create another subscription.
